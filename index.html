Who we are

The Caltech Entrepreneurship Club aims to provide aspiring Caltech entrepreneurs with the knowledge and connections to help launch the companies of the future. Our myriad of events include hosting business plan competitions, entrepreneurship bootcamps, field trips to Silicon Valley and San Diego, the Entrepreneurship Seminar Series which brings successful entrepreneurs to campus, and the Startup Workshop Series which provides detailed information on legal matters and other logistics. We also have close relationships with Caltech's Office of Technology Transfer, Idealab, the Pasadena Angels, SoCalBio,TechZulu, both USC and UCLA business schools, and other groups interested in fostering entrepreneurship. We hope to build an entrepreneurial spirit at Caltech.

How to join

If you would like to join our mailing list and receive updates on upcoming events / opportunities go here.
